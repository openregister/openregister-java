About registers

A register is a list of data that comes from a reliable source and is the only list of its kind.

Each register is made up of records. Each record is made up of ‘fields’ and ‘values’. When any of the information in a register changes, a new entry is added to the record. The most recent entry is shown as the current version of the record. Older entries are also kept as part of the record to show when it was changed.

You can find more information about registers on GOV.UK and view all registers.
